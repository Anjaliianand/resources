Automated Agricultural Monitoring

Integrated into farm monitoring systems, PlantCare AI enables continuous, real-time crop health surveillance. Captures leaf imagery, classifies diseases instantly, and generates detailed diagnostic reports — reducing manual workload and improving yield outcomes at scale.

A consumer-facing mobile application empowers home gardeners to photograph affected leaves and receive instant, expert-level diagnosis. No agronomist required — just point, shoot, and act. MobileNetV2's efficiency ensures fast on-device or cloud inference.
